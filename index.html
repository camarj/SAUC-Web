About Us

Mission

To be the best alternative for the commercialization, logistics operation and distribution in the international market of fresh fruits and fresh vegetables, tropical fruit pulps, shrimp, octopus, hearts of palm, organic coffee and confectionery, with the option of packing with own brand, ensuring our clients security, trust and transparency in our processes.

Vision

By 2028 we will be a solid, profitable and globalized company. Recognized for our level of service, products with high quality standards, competitive prices and for providing comprehensive, timely and tailored solutions; with a committed and result-oriented work team

Services

We work seriously and professionally to build and strengthen alliances that allow us to develop business in a sustainable and long-term way, based on transparency, legality and efficiency.

We work hand in hand with farmers and producers, thus guaranteeing fresh products of the highest quality

Frozen shrimps, prawns and octopus, with monitoring of the cold chain (fishing, production, packaging, storage and transportation) ensuring that the products do not lose their properties

Our fruit pulps are made with the best selection of tropical fruits (mango, blackberry, strawberry, soursop, lulo, etc.), 100% natural, without additives or preservatives

SAUC Inversiones SAS is an international product trading company, dedicated to the introduction of the products of our suppliers in the various international markets, as well as the location of products and representation in several South American countries (Colombia, Chile, Ecuador, Venezuela)

We locate suppliers, manufacturers and/or products according to the buyer's needs, both for purchasing and manufacturing, always with high quality standards and competitive prices.

Products

Fruits pulps

It is the edible part of the fruit, and they are obtained by a process of extraction and sieving of fresh and ripe fruits, clean and free of toxic pesticides, The pulp has color, flavor, aroma and nutrients of the fruit from which it is extracted , and they are the ideal base for the preparation of ice creams, desserts, jams, yogurts, smoothies, among others.

Shrimps

Meat appreciated worldwide, for its soft and rich texture, low in calories, high in nutrients, contains vitamin A, B6, B12 and E, selenium, calcium, magnesium, manganese, copper, zinc, sodium, phosphorus, potassium, iron and Omega 3

Palm hearts

They are extracted from the palms, mainly from the coconut palm trees, white in color. It contains many nutritional properties, it has fiber, vitamin B6 and C, calcium, niacin, phosphorus, potassium and zinc. Helps to strengthen the immune system and digestive system, helps prevent heart disease.

Prawns

Considered a superfood, because they are low in grass, a source of protein and Omega 3, which help prevent mental illness. Contains B3, B9, B12, Magnesium, Selenium, and Sodium.

Octopus

In addition to being exquisite, the meat contains a high presence of macro and micro nutrients, which makes it desirable to meet the requirements of our body. It has a white and tender meat, with a mild almost sweet taste, Contains potassium, calcium, iron, magnesium and vitamins of group B (folic acid). Octopus is one of the few shellfish that does not offer purines in the body.

Fruits

They are those edible products obtained from cultivated or wild plants, with a mostly sweet taste, and with a great variety of sizes, smells, colors and textures. They have a high content of water and nutrients, help the optimal functioning of the digestive system. (blackberry, strawberry, mango, lulo, guava, soursop, pitaya, passion fruit, etc.)

Vegetables

Vegetables extracted from plants grown in the best fertile and nutrient-rich lands in South America. The original meaning is still commonly used and applied to plants collectively to refer to all edible plant matter. These vegetables can be eaten raw or cooked and play an important role in human nutrition as they are mostly low in fat and carbohydrates, but high in vitamins, minerals, and dietary fiber.
