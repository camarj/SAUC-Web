Products

Fruits pulps

The fruit pulps are the edible part of the fruit, and they are obtained by a process of extraction and sieving of fresh and ripe fruits, clean and free of toxic pesticides. The fruit pulps keep has color, flavor, aroma and nutrients of the original fruit, and they are the ideal base for the preparation of ice creams, desserts, jams, yogurts, smoothies, among others.

Shrimps

The shrimp's meat appreciated worldwide, for its soft and rich texture, low in calories, high in nutrients, contains vitamin A, B6, B12 and E, selenium, calcium, magnesium, manganese, copper, zinc, sodium, phosphorus, potassium, iron and Omega 3

Palm hearts

The palm heart is extracted from the palms, mainly from the coconut palm trees, white. It contains many nutritional properties, it has fiber, vitamin B6 and C, calcium, niacin, phosphorus, potassium and zinc. Helps to strengthen the immune system and digestive system, also helps prevent heart disease.

Prawns

Prawn meats are considered a superfood because they are low in the grass, a source of protein and Omega 3, which help prevent mental illness. Contains vitamin B3, B9 and B12, Magnesium, Selenium, and Sodium.

Octopus

The octopus meat is exquisite, contains a high presence of macro and micronutrients, which makes it desirable for the requirements of our body. It has a white and tender meat, with a mild almost sweet taste, Contains potassium, calcium, iron, magnesium and vitamin B´s (folic acid). Octopus is one of the few shellfish that does not offer purines in the body

Fruits

The farmers cultivate fruits, with a mostly sweet taste and with a great variety of sizes, smells, colors and textures. The fruits have a high content of water and nutrients, help the optimal functioning of the digestive system. (blackberry, strawberry, mango, lulo, guava, soursop, pitaya, passion fruit, etc.)

Vegetables
